Cheeseburger


who doesn't love a good cheeseburger this is an american classic. In this recipe I will teach you how to make one that will be juicy and delicious

Ingredients

  • meat
  • buns
  • cheese
  • tomatoes
  • lettuce
  • egg
  • salt and pepper
  • bread crumbs

Steps to make

  1. we are cooking these babies on the grill, go set the grill temp to around 400 degrees
  2. while the grill is pre heating take your meat and add 1 egg to it and 1/4 cup of bread crumbs, some salt and pepper
  3. form your burgers into four patties
  4. throw your patties onto the grill once it is ready, leave on each side for 4 minutes, only flip them once!
